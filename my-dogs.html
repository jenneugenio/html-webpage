Spark and Koda

I love my pups, Spark and Koda. Spark is 4 years old and Koda is 3 years old. We got both of them from Craigslist and although they aren't rescues, we feel as if they were because their previous owners did not take care of them like we do.

They are both chihuahua terriers and fairly small.

Spark and Koda love to play. Spark loves playing with the tennis ball and Koda loves running and being chased. They love laying in the sun and going for walks. Of course, they LOVE food and treats.

They are the best dogs (even if they have accidents sometimes) and I couldn't imagine life without them.
